Graphic Design

Sanson Estate Project

A luxury winery brochure concept blending artisanal credibility with refined typography and rich vineyard imagery to reflect the farm-to-table ethos.

The tri-fold layout uses warm gold tones, bottle photography, and ornamental borders to create a polished winery identity. Product messaging, location details, and supporting imagery are arranged to feel informative without losing the premium tone.
