Drawing Study 001

Magical Motion Study

This piece explores character energy through pose, costume, and added light effects. The floating chair, lifted hat, and curved pink trails create a sense of motion around the figure while keeping the character as the main focus.

The composition uses a simple background so the viewer can read the gesture clearly. The glow effects add a fantasy tone, while the diagonal body position and extended legs make the illustration feel active instead of static.
