Drawing Study 002

Interior Concept Study

This drawing develops a compact bedroom environment from two angles, focusing on lighting, structure, and visual storytelling. The room is designed with layered furniture, a lofted bed, and carefully placed props to make the space feel lived in.

The second view introduces a glowing character presence, shifting the same environment into a more narrative scene. Together, the studies show how perspective and light can change the mood of one setting.
