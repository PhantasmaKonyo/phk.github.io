Drawing Study 005

Floral Dress Concept

This concept sheet develops a formal character outfit through front and back views, accessory details, and floral motifs. The design blends fashion illustration with character design to show how the garment works as a complete visual identity.

The cream, rose, and gold palette gives the dress a soft romantic tone. Repeated flowers, ribbon shapes, and decorative panels create continuity across the full look.
