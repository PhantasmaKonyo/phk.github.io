Drawing Study 003

Art Censorship Poster

This poster uses illustration and direct copy to comment on the impact of censorship in creative spaces. The red marks crossing both the artwork and the artist create a visual link between silencing the piece and silencing the person who made it.

The restrained palette keeps the message focused, while the large headline gives the design an advocacy-poster structure. It is built to be readable quickly while still carrying emotional weight.
