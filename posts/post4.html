A New Chapter at HomeSense

Today marks a new chapter for me: starting my new job at HomeSense. It is a furniture and home goods store, but to me it also feels like a place full of visual ideas. Everywhere I look, there are textures, colors, patterns, displays, and little design choices that can teach me something.

As someone working toward becoming a graphic designer and animator, I see this job as more than a step in my work life. It is part of my creative journey. A store like HomeSense is built around atmosphere, layout, and the way people connect with objects. That kind of environment can sharpen my eye for composition, branding, color palettes, and the emotional side of design.

I am excited to learn from the inspiring things the store has to offer, from furniture arrangements to seasonal displays to the small details that make a space feel welcoming. I am also looking forward to meeting amazing customers and seeing how different people respond to style, comfort, and creativity in their own homes.

This opportunity feels like a reminder that creative growth does not only happen at a desk or inside design software. It can happen while helping someone find the right piece for their space, noticing how colors work together, or paying attention to how a display tells a story. I am grateful for this new beginning, and I am excited to see how it helps shape the designer and animator I am becoming.
