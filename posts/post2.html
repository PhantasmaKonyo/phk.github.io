Starting Small, Dreaming Big

May 28, 2025

I never thought building my first portfolio site would make me feel so... proud and terrified at the same time. Just a few weeks ago, I was sitting in bed (probably too late at night), wondering if I was even cut out for this creative industry.

But here I am — with a working site, my own custom blog, and a real job that I landed doing what I love. I've already been through an intense internship, designed for a real estate brand, and I'm currently running social media for a trampoline park (yup).

It's not always easy. Some days I feel like I'm barely keeping up. But I keep reminding myself: you don't need to be perfect — just brave enough to keep showing up.

This site is more than a portfolio. It's proof that I've already started. I'll look back on this post someday — and smile at how far I've come.

Thanks for being here.
