Starting Small, Dreaming Big

I never thought building my first portfolio site would make me feel so... proud and terrified at the same time. Just a few weeks ago, I was sitting in bed, probably too late at night, wondering if I was even cut out for this creative industry.

But here I am, with a working site, my own custom blog, and a real job that I landed doing what I love. I have already been through an intense internship, designed for a real estate brand, and worked in social media for a trampoline park.

It is not always easy. Some days I feel like I am barely keeping up. But I keep reminding myself: you do not need to be perfect, just brave enough to keep showing up.

This site is more than a portfolio. It is proof that I have already started. I will look back on this post someday and smile at how far I have come.

Thanks for being here.
