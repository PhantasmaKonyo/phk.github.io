My Time at Aerosports Windsor & St. Catharines

One of the most meaningful recent chapters in my journey was working as a social media manager for Aerosports Windsor and St. Catharines. I had the chance to help share the brand story, engage with the community, and create content that represented the energy and fun of the team.

It was a great experience that helped me grow creatively and professionally, and I am grateful for the time I spent there. That chapter has now come to an end as the organization has moved forward by relocating the team overseas.
